rules
  • Exact score: 3 points.
  • Correct result: 1 point if you pick the right winner, or the right draw.
  • Scorers: 1 point for each correct scorer, max 3 per match.
  • Scorer order does not matter. Pick the people, not the minute.
  • Boost country: pick one country. Games involving that country count double.
  • Boost applies to the whole match: exact score, result, and scorer points.
  • If your boost country is eliminated, the boost is done too.
  • Knockout draws: if your score is tied, choose who advances.
  • Leaderboard: total points first; exact scores are the first tiebreaker, then scorer points.
